Our Commitment to Freshness

From Fresh Crop to Fresh Roast, Peak Flavor is Our Promise

What "Fresh" Means to Us

Whether you are a professional roaster seeking the best green beans or a coffee lover looking for the perfect cup, freshness is paramount. It defines the vibrancy, aroma, and flavor potential of your coffee. At Fidus Achates, we've engineered our entire supply chain around two distinct but equally important journeys to guarantee that potential is delivered to you, every time.

Two Paths to Peak Flavor

The Green Bean Journey

For Roasters and Professionals

Current Crop Sourcing

We only source beans from the most recent harvest, ensuring you receive "fresh crop" coffee with maximum vitality and flavor potential.

Optimal Drying & Resting

At origin, beans are carefully dried to a stable 10-12% moisture content, a critical step for preserving quality during transit and storage.

Protective Transit

Your green beans are shipped in GrainPro bags to protect them from moisture, oxygen, and outside odors, locking in their unique terroir.

Climate-Controlled Storage

Upon arrival, we store all green coffee in a climate-controlled environment to maintain its quality until the moment you place your order.

Immediate Dispatch

When you order, we promptly package and dispatch your green beans, ensuring they get to your roastery quickly and in perfect condition.

The Roasted Bean Journey

For Cafes and Home Brewers

Order Received

Your order triggers the process. We don't pull from stock; we prepare to roast your coffee specifically for you.

Roasted to Order

Our roasters select the right green beans and roast them in small, precise batches to meet the demands of your order.

Optimal Degassing

We rest the freshly roasted coffee for the perfect amount of time, allowing flavors to develop and mature before packaging.

Sealed for Freshness

Your coffee is immediately packed in one-way valve bags, preventing oxygen from entering while letting CO2 escape.

Rapid Dispatch

Within 24-48 hours of roasting, your coffee is on its way to your door, ensuring it arrives within its peak flavor window.
