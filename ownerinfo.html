Meet Our Founder

Mr. Rutvik Meshram

Founder & CEO

With the mind of an engineer and the heart of a coffee connoisseur, Rutvik founded Fidus Achates International to bridge the world of precision with the art of specialty coffee. His journey from engineering blueprints to coffee beans is driven by a singular passion: to deliver unparalleled quality and build transparent, lasting relationships from farm to cup.

He believes that great coffee is not an accident. It's the result of a perfectly designed system of trust, quality control, and a deep respect for both the farmer's craft and the roaster's needs.

The Engineer's Approach to Coffee

Precision in Sourcing

We don't just find coffee; we analyze it. Rutvik's engineering mindset drives us to evaluate every variable—from terroir and altitude to processing methods—to source beans with specific, consistent, and exceptional flavor profiles.

Systematic Quality Control

Our quality assurance is a rigorous, repeatable process. Every lot undergoes systematic cupping and grading, ensuring that what you order is exactly what you receive, every single time.

Building Robust Partnerships

We see our relationships with farmers and roasters as critical systems. By ensuring transparency, fair pricing, and clear communication, we build a strong, efficient, and trustworthy supply chain.

A Message from Rutvik

"Fidus Achates—meaning 'faithful friend' in Latin—is more than just our name; it's our promise. A promise to be a faithful friend to the dedicated farmers we partner with, and to you, our discerning clients. My goal is to create a seamless connection between the two, built on a foundation of integrity, quality, and a shared passion for incredible coffee. Thank you for joining us on this journey."
