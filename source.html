The Heart of Indian Coffee

Our Sourcing Journey in Chikkamagaluru, Karnataka

The Cradle of Coffee in India

Nestled in the Western Ghats, Chikkamagaluru is more than just a location; it's the birthplace of Indian coffee. Legend tells of the saint Baba Budan planting the first seven coffee seeds here in the 17th century, starting a legacy that we are proud to continue.

The region's high altitude, rich soil, and unique microclimate create the perfect terroir for growing both Arabica and Robusta beans with complex and distinctive flavor profiles. We partner directly with estates that have perfected their craft over generations, ensuring every bean tells a story of this historic land.

Why We Source from Chikkamagaluru

Historic Terroir

Grown at altitudes of 1000-1500 meters in the Baba Budan Giri hills, the beans develop a unique sweetness and complexity found nowhere else.

Rich Biodiversity

Our coffees are shade-grown under a canopy of native trees alongside spices like pepper and cardamom, which enrich the soil and impart subtle notes to the final cup.

Masterful Processing

Our farm partners are experts in both washed (Plantation) and natural (Cherry) processing, meticulously controlling each step to highlight the inherent qualities of the beans.

Our Direct Trade Philosophy

Building Relationships

We go beyond transactions. We build long-term, personal relationships with our partner estates, visiting them regularly to collaborate and ensure mutual success.

Commitment to Quality

From selective hand-picking of only ripe red cherries to meticulous quality control and cupping at the source, we are obsessed with every detail that contributes to a superior cup.

Sustainable & Ethical Practices

We champion sustainable farming methods that preserve the region's precious biodiversity and ensure fair compensation, improving the livelihoods of the farming communities.
